Sponsorship

Minimum $5 CAD per sponsorship placement per page (for both english and french) per month. Ammobin supports both english and french. Can switch ads based on vistor's locale. Images must be relevant to firearms in Canada and must be approved by Ammobin. Images must be provided beforehand and will be served from Ammobin's cdn. They can be either a PNG or a GIF. Ads must link directly to your site. Multiple image sizes are allowed (see picture element). If so desired, a sendBeacon or ping can be sent to an endpoint on your domain (no thirdparties).
Front page images are 120x600. Images on calibre list pages and listings pages are 468x60
